📋 About This Recruitment

Mahanadi Coalfields Limited (MCL), a Mini Ratna Category-I PSU and subsidiary of Coal India Limited, has announced 500 vacancies for Assistant Foreman (Electrical) and Electrician Trainee posts. MCL operates in Odisha and is one of India's top coal producing companies. This is a rare opportunity for ITI and Diploma holders in Electrical trade to get a permanent central government PSU job with excellent salary under the NCWA-XI (National Coal Wage Agreement) structure — one of the best wage scales for trade workers in India.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
Q. What is MCL?
Mahanadi Coalfields Limited (MCL) is a Mini Ratna Category-I PSU under Coal India Limited. It operates coal mines in Odisha and is headquartered in Sambalpur.
Q. Can ITI pass candidates apply for Assistant Foreman?
No. Assistant Foreman requires a 3-year Diploma in Electrical Engineering. ITI candidates can only apply for the Electrician Trainee post.
Q. Is there an interview in MCL recruitment?
No. There is no interview. Selection is based on written test followed by trade test and medical examination only.
Q. What is NCWA-XI salary?
NCWA (National Coal Wage Agreement) is the wage structure for non-executive coal mine workers. NCWA-XI is the latest revision. Assistant Foreman basic pay starts at ₹47,330/month.
Q. Can candidates from outside Odisha apply?
Yes. This is a central PSU recruitment open to candidates from all states of India. No domicile restriction for most posts.
