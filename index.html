UPSC Civil Services (IAS/IPS/IFS)
India's most prestigious exam. Salary starts at ₹56,100/month.
  • Starting salary ₹56,100 + allowances
  • Government bungalow & official car
  • Lifetime pension after retirement
  • Power to make policy decisions
  • Highest respect & social status
  • 3 attempts for UR, 6 for OBC, 9 for SC/ST
📋
SSC CGL (Staff Selection Commission)
Most popular central govt exam for graduates. 30+ lakh apply every year.
  • Salary ₹25,500 – ₹1,12,400/month
  • Central govt job security for life
  • HRA + DA + Transport allowance
  • Medical benefits for entire family
  • Pension + gratuity on retirement
  • Posting across India
🏦
IBPS / SBI Banking Exams
PO, Clerk, SO roles in public banks. Best work-life balance.
  • Salary ₹23,700 – ₹63,840/month
  • Home loan at extremely low interest
  • Medical insurance for family
  • Annual increments guaranteed
  • Pension after retirement
  • Saturday-Sunday holidays!
🚂
Railway RRB Exams
India's largest employer. Free rail passes for family!
  • Salary ₹18,000 – ₹1,12,400/month
  • Free railway travel passes (family)
  • Free/subsidized quarters
  • Free medical at Railway hospitals
  • Children's education pass
  • Zero layoff policy
⚔️
Defence Exams (NDA/CDS/AFCAT)
Serve as Army, Navy, Air Force officer. Most respected career.
  • Salary ₹56,100 – ₹2,50,000/month
  • Free accommodation always
  • Best medical facilities in India
  • Canteen at highly subsidized rates
  • Pension + disability benefits
  • Highest respect in society
🗺️
State PSC Exams (MPSC/RPSC/UPPSC)
State officer roles. Posting in home state. Great lifestyle.
  • Salary ₹44,900 – ₹1,42,400/month
  • Posting in your home state
  • Government housing facility
  • Full medical benefits
  • Pension after service
  • Local influence & community impact
