Privacy Policy 🔒
We respect your privacy. Simple and honest.

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how HireIndia ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects any information you provide when you use this website. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Effective Date: May 2026  |  Website: hireindia.in

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide our service:

  • Usage data: Pages visited, time spent, device type (via analytics tools like Google Analytics)
  • Saved jobs: Stored locally on your device using browser storage — we do not collect this on our servers
  • Contact form: If you email us, we store your email to respond to your enquiry only

We do NOT collect: Aadhaar numbers, bank details, passwords, or any sensitive personal information.

How We Use Your Information

  • To understand how users use the site and improve it
  • To respond to your queries and feedback
  • We do NOT sell, share, or trade your data with third parties for marketing

Cookies

We may use standard browser cookies to improve your experience (e.g. remembering your dark/light mode preference). These cookies do not store personal information. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

Third-Party Links

HireIndia contains links to official government websites and recruitment portals. Once you leave our website, we are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. Always verify you are on the official government domain before entering any personal information.

Your Rights

You have the right to request deletion of any personal data we hold about you. Email us at contact@hireindia.in and we will respond within 7 days.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the site means you accept the updated policy.
