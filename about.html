Who We Are

HireIndia is a free government job discovery platform built for every Indian job seeker — from rural villages to metro cities. We believe every qualified candidate deserves equal access to government job information, without paywalls, spam, or fake alerts.

We are a small independent team passionate about making government job information simple, honest, and accessible. HireIndia is not affiliated with any government body, ministry, or official recruitment agency.

Our Mission

Millions of Indians prepare for government exams every year. Yet most job portals are cluttered, unreliable, and filled with outdated information. HireIndia exists to fix that.

  • Provide 100% accurate job information sourced directly from official notifications
  • Show complete details — salary, age limit, eligibility, selection process — all in one place
  • Be honest about what we have and what's coming soon
  • Stay completely free for all job seekers, always

What We Do

Every job listing on HireIndia is:

  • 📋 Sourced from the official recruitment notification
  • 🔗 Linked back to the official government/organisation website
  • ✅ Verified for accuracy before publishing
  • 📅 Updated with real deadlines and exam dates

We do not post unverified rumours, predicted vacancies, or unofficial information.

Contact & Feedback

We are a growing platform and value your feedback. Found an error? Want to suggest a job? Reach out to us on our Contact page. We respond within 24 hours.
