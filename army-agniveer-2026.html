📋 About Agniveer Scheme

The Agniveer scheme is the Indian Army's short-service recruitment program introduced by the Government of India. Under this scheme, young candidates are recruited for a 4-year service period in the Indian Army. After 4 years, 25% of Agniveers are retained as regular soldiers based on merit and vacancy. The remaining 75% receive a Seva Nidhi package of approximately ₹11.71 lakh (tax free) and a skill certificate to help them in civilian careers.
