Important information about how HireIndia works
⚠️ HireIndia is NOT an official government portal

Not an Official Portal

HireIndia (hireindia.in) is an independent, private information platform. It is NOT affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to:

  • The Government of India or any State Government
  • Any Ministry, Department, or PSU
  • UPSC, SSC, Railways, or any recruitment board
  • Indian Army, Navy, Air Force, or any defence organisation

For official recruitment information, always visit the official organisation website.

How We Verify Jobs

Every job posted on HireIndia is verified through the following process:

  • ✅ Official recruitment notification (PDF) is located on the organisation's official website
  • ✅ Key details (vacancies, salary, dates, eligibility) are cross-checked with the notification
  • ✅ Apply link points directly to the official website — never a third-party portal
  • ✅ Information is reviewed before publishing

Despite our best efforts, information may occasionally be outdated or contain errors. Always verify from the official notification before applying.

No Fees — Ever

HireIndia never charges any fee from job seekers for accessing job information, applying, or any other purpose. All application fees (if any) are as defined in the official notification and paid directly on the official recruitment portal.

🚨 Beware of fraud: If anyone claims to be from HireIndia and asks for money in exchange for a job, it is a scam. Report it to us and to your local police immediately.

External Links

HireIndia contains links to official government and organisation websites. We are not responsible for the content, accuracy, or security of external websites. Always check the URL before entering personal information — official government sites end in .gov.in, .nic.in, .org.in or the organisation's own domain.

Limitation of Liability

HireIndia and its team shall not be held liable for:

  • Any decision made based on information from this website
  • Missed application deadlines
  • Any loss arising from use or inability to use this website

Use this platform as a starting point for research, not as the final authority. Always refer to the official notification.
