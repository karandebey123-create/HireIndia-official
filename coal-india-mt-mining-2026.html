📋 About This Recruitment

Coal India Limited (CIL), India's largest coal producing Maharatna PSU, has released a recruitment notification for 276 Management Trainee (Mining) posts through GATE-2025 scores. This is separate from the non-mining MT recruitment. Candidates who appeared in GATE-2025 in Mining Engineering are eligible to apply. Starting salary is ₹60,000 per month in E-1 grade — one of the best paying PSU entry-level positions in India.
