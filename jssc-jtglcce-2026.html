❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
Q. What is JSSC JTGLCCE full form?
JSSC JTGLCCE stands for Jharkhand Staff Selection Commission Junior Technical Grade Level Combined Competitive Examination.
Q. Is Jharkhand domicile mandatory?
Yes. JSSC exams are primarily for Jharkhand domicile candidates. You must have a valid Jharkhand domicile certificate to apply.
Q. Is there negative marking?
Yes. 1/3 mark is deducted for each wrong answer. Do not attempt questions you are unsure about.
Q. Is there an interview?
No interview. Selection is based entirely on the written examination marks.
Q. Can Diploma holders apply for Junior Engineer posts?
Yes! Both Degree (B.E./B.Tech) and 3-year Diploma holders in relevant engineering disciplines are eligible for Junior Engineer posts.
Q. What is the application fee for female candidates?
All female candidates are exempted from application fee. They can apply completely free of charge.
